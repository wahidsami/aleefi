AI technology utilizes vast amounts of data related to pet health and disease symptoms to identify patterns and trends that may indicate potential diseases or health issues. 

By analyzing subtle changes in behavior, eating habits, or physiological parameters, AI can detect diseases in pets at an early stage, allowing for prompt intervention and treatment.
